Bio

Ysabel Morales is a graphic designer, artist, and programmer based in Los Angeles, previously Portland, Oregon. She codes her own work (not full stack, but enough to make it look good), binds her own books, and screenprints her own posters.


BFA in Graphic Design from Otis College of Art and Design, where her thesis — The Companion Bible — is held in the library's Special Collections. Freelancing since 2018 across independent artists, writers, restaurants, skincare brands, and LGBTQ+ organizations. Currently producing digital and print brand assets at Flight ID.


When she's not on her computer, she's at a new restaurant or at home in bed.
