The Companion Bible


Limited print Edition 1 of 3 held in the Otis College's library's Special Collections. A BFA thesis exploring my own religious deconstruction through print. Six handbound works created using coptic stitching, screenprinting and ASCII art overlays to examine our inherited faith and lived experiences. Each work takes inspiration from different formats: devotional book, voter pamphlet, recipe card etc...

Summaeverythang


A rebrand for an organization providing mutual aid to South Central LA and Watts. I moved the identity away from polished sterile aesthetics toward the visual language already alive in the community: stickers, crates, spray paint, cardboard. The system uses the food supply chain as inspiration, layered textures, and the banana as a symbol of nourishment to create a flexible identity that grows with the organization and helps its community to grow.

One of four posters selected from Otis College for an official Olympics poster competition, celebrating the cultural bond between Los Angeles and Paris ahead of the 2028 Olympics. The design itself connects both cities through iconic landmarks and imagery and also serves as a reference to Flash Invader's street art mosaics. Currently exhibited in both Paris and LA.

Harmonic Horizons


A visual and tactile translation of electronic sound that celebrates our 3 main senses. While festival branding is traditionally auditory-led, this system centers the triad of sight, hearing, and touch. By mapping sonic frequencies to physical textures and inviting visuals, the identity creates a visually loud and tangible anchor for an otherwise immaterial experience.
